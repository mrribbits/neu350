Sample data (sub-999) collected on 03/04/25 at Skyra.
A T1w and two functional runs, processed through fMRIPrep.

The radialstimulus run is a large full-field rotating checkboard, block design, 20 sec on/off for 4 minutes.
The fullfieldstimulus run is a flickering checkerboard encompassing the entire screen, 20 sec on/off for 4 minutes.
